How To Play

CARD DESCRIPTIONS

Student

Each day round you attempt to figure out the idenity of the Death Eaters in your midst. By majority vote, you choose one person each day to be sent to Azkaban in hopes of catching a Deth Eater.


Legilimens

You have the power to see one player's true idenity per night.
Wins with the Students.


The Dark Lord

Disguised as a Death Eater, you awaken fo the Death Eater meetings and vote on who to kill. Then, you may awaken once more to choose another victim(anyone, even Death Eaters).
Wins if you are the only player left.


Crabbe & Goyle

You must always argue in favor of sending a player to Azkaban.
Wins with the Students.


Elder Wand

You may kill one player. To use your power. hold up one finger for the moderator to see when called upon in the night round.
Wins with the Students.


Felix Felicis

At the begining of each night round, choose a different player to bestow luck upon. That player cannot be killed for the remainder of the night phase.
Wins with the Students.


Headmaster

Your vote counts 2x when voting to send a player to Azkaban.
Wins with the Students.


The Invisiblity Cloak

The Invisiblity Cloak allows you to spy on the Death Eaters during their meeting. You may open your eyes while they are meeting, but be careful! If you are caught, they will probably decide to kill you! (You may not spy on the Dark Lord.)
Wins with the Students.


Death Eater

The Death Eaters have the power to kill one person each night, determined by majority vote. During the day, players will attempt to identify the Death Eaters and send them to Azkaban.


Marked by the Dark Lord

Death Eaters and the Dark Lord cannot kill you during the night(much like Voldemort is unable to kill Harry many times. You can, however be killed or sent to Azkaban at any other time.
Wins with the Students.


The Master of Death

You have two powers. Each may be used once during the night round. Resurrection Stone: You may save one player from the Death Eaters or Dark Lord. Hold up one finger for the moderator to see. Elder Wand: You may kill one player. Hold up two fingers and inficate who is to die.


Portkey

Once per game at the begining of the night round, choose another player. You and that player are removed from play for that night and the next day round. While removed from play, you are safe from the Death Eaters, Dark Lord, and Azkaban but not the effets of the Potions Master. You may not participate in discussions or peek during night rounds.

Wins with the Students.

Potions Master

At the begining of the game, you brew a love potion and give it to two players. These lovers are connected for the rest of the game. The lovers may not accuse on another at any poin, and when one lover is killed the other dies as well.They win with respective cards or together.
Wins with the Students.


Resurrection Stone

You may save one player from the Death Eaters or Dark Lord. To use your power, hold up one finger for the moderator to see when called upon in the night round.
Wins with the Students.


Auror

When you die, you accidentally set off your wand and bring another player with you into death.
Wins with the Students.


Dolores Umbridge

Until you are killed or sent to Azkaban each day round.
Wins with the Students.
